GET STARTED

WHO WE ARE

We are making software which is based on the latest achievements in mobile and server development. Our specialization is to make not alone-standing products but platforms, that is offering bigger opportunities for modern businesses.

We are company where knows that new technology makes peoples lives easier and business more competitive.

 

 

WHAT WE DO

SERVICESONFLY

Delivery/Courier, Moving, Security and any other service that is based on geolocation of client sides and administration of all the processes by server side.

 

GOODSONFLY

Selling or Renting Real Estate, Cars, Boats, any other stuff, even from your neighbors. Also all this is linked to the geolocation.

 

GETONFLY

Solutions that can be provided on this platform: aggregation of proposals on food/grocery/clothes/anything else delivery from restaurants/shops, etc.

 

ActivitiesOnFlY

Solutions that can be provided on this platform: any content providing from business/people who have something to say and it can be sold.

FinanceOnFly

Solutions that can be provided on this platform: peer-to-peer loans; gathering money from users for any purpose (charity, event, project, etc.) with/without following transfer; payment system.

 

OUR MISSION

We are doing useful things. For business which is entering new level of evolution, thanks to our solutions and services. And for end-users, making their lives easier.

This is our Mission. And thanks of our consumers is our reward.
